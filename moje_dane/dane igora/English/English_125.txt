[263] Because the two licenses, GFDL and Creative Commons, were incompatible, in November 2008, following the request of the project, the Free Software Foundation (FSF) released a new version of the GFDL designed specifically to allow Wikipedia to relicense its content to CC BY-SA by August 1, 2009.

(A new version of the GFDL automatically covers Wikipedia contents.)

In April 2009, Wikipedia and its sister projects held a community-wide referendum which decided the switch in June 2009.
